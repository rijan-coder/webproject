Report an Issue or Send Feedback

Your feedback is crucial for maintaining and improving the quality of our service. Please use the form below to report issues such as localized power outages, faulty meters, billing discrepancies, or safety hazards related to electric infrastructure. If you are reporting an emergency (like a downed power line), please call our toll-free hotline immediately instead of using this form. For all other non-urgent matters, filling out this form ensures your inquiry is directed to the correct department within the NEA. Please include your distribution branch and account number in the message section for faster processing.
