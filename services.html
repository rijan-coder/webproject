Essential Services for Our Valued Customers

NEA is dedicated to providing convenient and efficient services to all our customers, whether residential, industrial, or commercial. Our services range from new electricity connection applications and meter installation to billing inquiries and maintenance requests. We have embraced digitalization to streamline processes, allowing customers to view and pay bills online through various platforms. Regular meter reading ensures accurate billing, and dedicated service centers handle localized issues promptly. Our goal is to minimize power disruptions and maintain a high standard of public service across all our operational areas.

How to Apply for a New Connection

  1. Submit application form to the nearest NEA distribution center.
  2. Provide required documents (e.g., land ownership certificate).
  3. Site inspection and technical assessment by NEA personnel.
  4. Payment of connection and meter installation fees.
